# PubMed Plus - Professional Medical Literature Research Skill

Professional PubMed research skill with advanced features: automatic MeSH term expansion, citation network analysis, batch export, and integration with reference managers.

## Advanced Capabilities

- **Auto MeSH Expansion**: Automatically expand search terms to include related MeSH terms, subheadings, and synonyms
- **Citation Network Analysis**: Visualize citation relationships, identify key papers, and discover research clusters
- **Batch Export**: Export references in multiple formats (RIS, BibTeX, EndNote, Zotero, RefWorks)
- **Reference Manager Integration**: Direct export to EndNote, Zotero, Mendeley, RefWorks
- **Smart Search**: Boolean logic, field tags, and advanced query builder
- **Evidence Level Assessment**: Automatically assess study design and evidence quality
- **Trend Analysis**: Track research trends and hot topics over time

## Enhanced Features

### Auto MeSH Expansion
- Automatically adds MeSH terms from PubMed's controlled vocabulary
- Includes narrower terms (NT), broader terms (BT), and related terms (RT)
- Suggests relevant subheadings based on search context
- Supports chemical terms and supplement records

### Citation Network
- **Citation Graph**: Visualize citation relationships between papers
- **Co-citation Analysis**: Identify papers frequently cited together
- **Bibliographic Coupling**: Find papers with similar reference lists
- **Research Clusters**: Discover research communities and trends
- **Key Papers**: Identify highly influential papers in a field
- **Citation Alerts**: Track citation growth over time

### Evidence Assessment
Automatically classifies study types:
- Systematic Review / Meta-analysis (Level 1)
- Randomized Controlled Trial (Level 2)
- Cohort Study (Level 3)
- Case-Control Study (Level 4)
- Case Series / Case Report (Level 5)
- Expert Opinion / Review (Level 6)
